About Google Shop

Google is not just about search, but it also offers a powerful platform for target marketing which can drive a lot of traffic to your Odoo website and lead to higher conversions. Google product feeds or Google shopping is one of the most effective advertising tools present at your disposal. The module is designed to allow you to create product feeds for your Odoo with your Google Merchant Center so you can send the product information from Odoo to your Google Merchant account.
Additionally, map the Odoo Product fields with that of Google fields to display product price, description, etc. correctly on Google shopping.Originally, the service listed prices submitted by merchants and was monetized through AdWords advertising like other Google services. However, Google announced that the service would shift in late-2012 to a paid model where merchants would have to pay the company in order to list their products on the service.

What is Google Shopping?

Google Shopping is one of the most targeted advertisement tools provided by Google itself. Google Shopping is the largest growth opportunity for most online retailers in 2019 (link >> https://searchengineland.com/google-shopping-is-largest-growth-opportunity-for-most-online-retailers-in-2019-309913 )
With Google shopping, your potential audience/visitors can increase ten-folds depending upon the scale of your business. By proper management, your products can come up in the results in organic searches from the customers for the targeted populace. It takes advantage of two platforms: Google Merchant account and Google Adwords.
With Google merchant you maintain your website information, including pictures and pricing, to be displayed in relevant Google Shopping searches. While Google Adwords is the advertisement platform for your products. Here, you can manage bidding, your budget, much more. Intuitively, hosting your products on Google Shopping can increase your customers and conversions.

Why Google Shop ??

Are you running an online web store where you are allowing your customers to search for products that you sell? If yes, you can use Google Shop to advertise your entire product catalog on the google shop where you have installed the Google Shop and add your odoo products to the Google Shop.
Suppose that we are having an online store of "Dresses" and we want our products to come on Google Searching. It will surely be a time-consuming process as one has to manage its products on his online store as well as on Google Shop. But, if we make use of the Google Shop and put all of our products in the Google shop Addons it would for sure that the Dresses will be sent to the google shop.. Thus reduces the time to sync the products to the google shop.

What is Google Merchant Account?

Google merchant account is your product control center for Google shopping Ads campaign. It is where you save product data from your store. You can manage your product data for your google ads.
Once you have set up your advertisement strategy using Google Adwords and other platforms, this data is then rendered as feeds and automatically picked by Google.
It is appropriately displayed as ads feed to the customers in accordance with their intent, behavior on the internet and search queries.
Prerequisites to configure Odoo Google Product module with Google Merchant Console To configure the module successfully, you would need the following:
  • A Google Developer Console account. This account is needed to obtain client Id and client secret for the configuration.
Click on the following link to create a Google Developer console account >> https://console.developers.google.com/
  • A Google Merchant Account to get a merchant ID. Google merchant account is also needed to sync your and upload your product details from Odoo.
CLICK HERE to create a new account.
Then paste the merchant ID in Odoo under account menu.
Follow the steps below to verify your website.
Generating Client ID and Client Secret on Google Developer console.
  • Once you have set up your Google Dev console account, click on credentials >> OAuth Client ID >> Web application.
  • Now, choose any name for the configuration.
  • Enter your website URL under field -"Authorized JavaScript origins"
  • next, copy the callback URL from account menu in Odoo and paste it under the field - "Authorized redirect URIs"
  • The client ID and client secret are automatically generated.
  • Once you have obtained the Client ID and Client secret key form the Google dev console, go to your Odoo backend.
  • Paste the client ID and Secret key in the account menu under Google product.

Verifying your Website with Google Merchant Account

  • If you have logged into your Merchant account for the first time you would need to verify your shop domain with Google.
  • Go to Business information >> Website.
  • Enter your website URL and then choose the "I have access to my server" option to verify your website. (You can also use any one of the other two options available.)
  • Then, click on "Upload an HTML file on my website".
  • Now, download the HTML file which is shown in the page under the steps. For example : google8156543780c743.html
  • Then, in Odoo, go to account >> Token 1. Here, under merchant details. Paste the entire name of the file next to the field - "URL to verify".
  • Now, open the file and paste the text inside it under the field- "Data in URL"
  • Go back to your Google merchant account and click on the URL in step 3 on that page. Something like this:- Confirm the upload by visiting https://demo.webkul.com/google8156540c7cf43.html.
  • Your URL should be verified and you can start to send and sync product information to Google Shopping.
Detailed Features List

Integrate GMC With Odoo

  • Configure Google Shopping with Odoo.
  • Sync your Google merchant account with Odoo.

One-Step Domain Verification With Google

  • Do away with the complex process of verifying your domain.
  • Separate field which allows you to verify domain in simple steps

Manage Product Prices With Odoo Pricelist

  • The price on Google Shopping ads can be managed Using Odoo pricelists

Upload Product Data To GMC

  • The Odoo product data can be saved to Google merchant center.
  • Product data can be sent individual product-wise or domain wise to the Google Merchant center.

Map Product Categories

  • You can map the product categories available in Odoo with those of defined Google Product categories.

Map Product Fields

  • Map the Odoo product fields with the those of Google Product Fields
  • You can also create custom fields for your Odoo products if same is not available on Google end.
