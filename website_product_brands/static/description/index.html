Odoo Website Product Brand

Odoo E-Commerce Plug-In For Brand Based Differentiation Of Product

Features:

  • Provides Brand as Identifier for your products.
  • Allows brand based Product differentiation.
  • User can Filter Product By Selecting Brands Form Attribute Filter Bar.
  • Brand Filtration also works well with default attribute filter like Nike (brand)+ 8 No. Shoes.
  • Option to Create and Manage the Brands from the back panel.
  • Your website user can use Brand Filter to select your products.
  • You can assign the brand to new created product as well all your previous store products.
  • It is compatible with all versions of Odoo.
  • It allow you to assign brand to thousand of product on just one click, it will save your time too.

Configuration

Create Brand

After the successful installation of the Website Product Brand module you will be able to see the Product Brand menu under Website.

Some Demo Brand are By default Provided in this module, But as per your need you can create the Product Brand under Product Brand menu.

Assign Brand to Multiple Products

This module provide the feature of assigning multiple product to a brand on just one click.

Website View

Filter Brand Based Products

On the website the user can filter the product with combination of attribute and brand.

Backend View

Products Grouped By Brand

In the Product menu the Admin can categorize the product based on their brand.

Product Brand Assignment

This module also provides the feature of assigning Individual brand to a product.
